# Trip - Plans

:::note[TL;DR]
Your trip is a list of plans, ordered by day and time. Filter, group, and customize the view to manage your itinerary effectively. Click a day for map highlight, or a row for details.
:::

The plans have different levels of information (see [Trip - Plan creation](trip-plan-creation)).

<img src="/trip/img/plan.png" alt="A plan" />
<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}><sup>A plan with a place, a status and an attachments</sup></div>

The plans panel support various interactions; actions bar at the top, clicking a day or a plan.

:::tip
The plans panel can be resized by sliding your mouse on its right edge
:::

### Actions bar

- **Bulk** actions, to edit, duplicate or delete multiple plans
- **Filter** plans and **select columns** to show (defaults pre-selected)
- **Highlight** the complete itinerary on the map
- **Export** the trip plans in a tailored print, a calendar file (_.ical_) or a CSV file

<br/>

:::tip
For accommodations and transport, I recommend using a double-entry approach, similar to accounting:

- Create one plan for the start (e.g., `00:01`)
- Create another plan for the end (e.g., `23:59`)

This helps you:

- Differentiate 'From' and 'To' locations easily
- Track nights at accommodations accurately
- Show continuous presence at a location across days using coordinates

<img src="/trip/img/plans_doubleentry.png" alt="Double-entry plans" />
<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}><sup>Double-entry plans</sup></div>

:::

### Day click

Click on a day to highlight a point-to-point route on the map and show its summary.

:::tip
You can display point-to-point route, the summary or the computed itinerary individually through the day's three dots button.
:::

### Plan click

Click any plan to open its details. The panel shows all plan info; more fields mean richer details. Attachments are downloadable.
