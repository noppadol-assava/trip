# Trip - Anonymous share

:::note[TL;DR]
Share your trip publicly via a unique read-only link. Others can view partial or full trip details without an account.  
The shared trip stays synchronized with your updates. You can revoke sharing anytime by removing it.
:::

Share your trip publicly by generating a unique URL. Anyone with this link can view your itinerary without needing an account, allowing them to explore your places, plans, notes, checklists, and packing lists in a read-only format.

The shared link stays fully synchronized with your account, meaning any updates you make to the trip will instantly reflect for anyone viewing it.

If you ever want to stop sharing your itinerary, simply delete the generated link to immediately revoke public access.

:::tip
You can choose between a base share or a complete share, which additionally grants viewers access to your trip attachments.
:::
