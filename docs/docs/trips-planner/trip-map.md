# Trip - Map

:::note[TL;DR]
The map displays your places, itineraries and routes.
:::

The map presents a visual overview of your trip by showing all places and itineraries, based on your plans.

<img src="/trip/img/trip_map.png" alt="Trip - Map section" />
<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}><sup>Trip - Map section</sup></div>

## Highlight

Depending on your needs, you can view a point-to-point route or a routing itinerary.

:::tip
Places on the map can be left-clicked for details and right-clicked for plan creation shortcut.
If the _place_ details is open and you right click on another one, it will display the routing between the two.
:::
