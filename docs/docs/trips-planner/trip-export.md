# Trip - Export and print

:::note[TL;DR]
You can export your trip in CSV, iCal or a formatted PDF.
:::

You can export your trip in multiple formats to suit your needs: CSV, iCal, and a nicely formatted PDF for printing or sharing.

- **CSV**
  Download your raw trip data as a comma-separated values file for easy spreadsheet management.

- **iCal (.ics)**
  Add your plans directly in your preferred calendar application to seamlessly integrate your travel schedule.

- **Pretty Print (.pdf)**
  Generate a beautifully formatted document of your plans and places, perfect for offline access, keeping a hard copy, or sharing with travel companions.
