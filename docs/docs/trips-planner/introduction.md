# Introduction

:::note[TL;DR]
Your next journey plans are organized in a list with a map view. Upload attachments, use the checklist and packing list to ease your planning. Collaborate with other members on your trip.
:::

Your plans are organized in a [list format](trip-plans) displayed next to the [map](trip-map) showing your places.

Beyond itinerary planning, it offers extra features to simplify preparation, including [attachments, checklist, packing list](trip-attachments-lists) and more.

Planning a group trip is nice, but planning together is better. You can [collaborate](trip-collaboration) with other users within your instance.
