# Trip - Archive

:::note[TL;DR]
Archive completed trips with an optional review summary. Archived trips are read-only, displayed at the end of your trips page, and you can unarchive them anytime to make edits.
:::

Once your trip is complete, you can archive it to keep your dashboard organized. The app will automatically generate a text summary of your itinerary, which you can edit and customize to reflect on your travel highlights, note future improvements, or record personal tips.

:::info[important]
Archived trips are read-only. If you ever need to make changes, you can simply unarchive the trip, update your details, and re-archive it at any time.
:::
